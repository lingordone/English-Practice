## Grammar Focus
- **Present Perfect Tense (现在完成时)**: Used to connect past actions to the present moment. Common in standups and deployment conversations when reporting what's been done.  
  - *"I've just deployed the new feature"* — 我刚部署了新功能（强调刚完成的结果）  
  - *"Have you run the full test suite yet?"* — 你跑完完整测试套件了吗？（询问截至现在的状态）  
  - *"Not yet — I haven't finished compiling."* — 还没有，我还没编译完。（否定形式）  
  
  Structure: **have/has + past participle**  
  - *"We've optimized the query."* — 我们已经优化了查询。  
  - *"She has integrated the branch."* — 她已经集成了分支。
