## Scenario Dialogue
> **Li Ming:** I wrote in my diary last night about my doubts regarding my major. Sometimes I wonder if I made the right choice.  
> **Zhang Wei:** Don't doubt yourself. Remember why you chose this path. Your dream is to become an engineer, right?  
> **Li Ming:** Yes, but the workload is draining me. I'm drowning in assignments, and I dread the upcoming exams.  
> **Zhang Wei:** That's normal for college life. Just don't drift away from your goal. Stay focused — like an eagle hunting its prey.  
> **Li Ming:** You're right. I'm eager to learn and improve. By the way, are you going to the drama club rehearsal?  
> **Zhang Wei:** Sure! After that we can grab a drink at the campus café. I bought a dozen cookies to share.  
> **Li Ming:** Sounds great. I need to finish the first draft of my essay first. Let's meet at 5 PM.  
> **Zhang Wei:** Deal! And make sure your clothes aren't dirty from the military drill training this morning!
