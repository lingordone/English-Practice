Sarah feels a little **guilt** for arriving late, but Li Ming says it is a common **habit** for newcomers. He keeps a **handy** campus map in his bag. Sarah is **happy** despite the **harsh** cold weather. In her **haste**, she almost trips, but Li Ming stops her **hasty** fall. The campus has tall **hedge** rows and a beekeeping club — Sarah's favorite **hobby** is making **honey** tea. Li Ming **heaves** her **heavy** suitcase up the stairs. "**Hello**!" a friend calls out. "We must meet the **guest** speaker soon; **hence**, let us hurry!" Sarah's **heart** fills with excitement as she **guesses** what adventure awaits.

  - √ *"It is raining; hence, the game is cancelled."* — 下雨了，所以比赛取消了。
