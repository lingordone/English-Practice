# Day 9 · CET-4 · 校园迎新

## Today's Scenario
> Li Ming is showing a new exchange student, Sarah, around the university campus. The security **guard** greets them warmly. Li Ming **guides** Sarah through the science building where biology students watch eggs **hatch** in the lab. Sarah feels a little **guilt** for arriving late, but Li Ming says it is a common **habit** for newcomers. He keeps a **handy** campus map in his bag. Sarah is **happy** despite the **harsh** cold weather. In her **haste**, she almost trips, but Li Ming stops her **hasty** fall. The campus has tall **hedge** rows and a beekeeping club — Sarah's favorite **hobby** is making **honey** tea. Li Ming **heaves** her **heavy** suitcase up the stairs. "**Hello**!" a friend calls out. "We must meet the **guest** speaker soon; **hence**, let us hurry!" Sarah's **heart** fills with excitement as she **guesses** what adventure awaits.

## Scenario Dialogue

> **Guard:** Hello! Please show your student ID.
> **Li Ming:** Sure! This is our new guest, Sarah from London.

> **Li Ming:** Look — the hedge garden and the beehives! They make honey here.
