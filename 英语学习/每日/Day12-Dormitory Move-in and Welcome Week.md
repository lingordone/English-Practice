# Day 12 · CET-4 · 宿舍入住与欢迎周

## 🎯 Today's Scenario
> *Sophia and Jason volunteer as orientation leaders during Welcome Week. They help students move into the dormitory—checking luggage limits, guiding a lorry to the lobby, handing out linen, and drinking a liter of water. A local restaurant delivers lunch. They lodge new students, use logic to solve room issues, fix a loose shelf on the lower bunk, and attend a magic show. Madam the dorm manager greets everyone. A health talk covers liver care. Jason is a loyal friend and a lover of books. They feel lucky to have this major experience. A manly effort carrying boxes!*
> *斯苏菲亚和杰森在欢迎周担任新生导航志愿者。他们帮助新生入住宿舍——检查行李限重、指导卡车到大堂、发放亚麻布、喝一升水。一家当地餐厅送来午餒。他们安顿新生、用逻辑解决房间问题、修理下铺的松动螺丝、参加魔术表演。宿管夫人招呼所有人。一场健康讲座涵盖肝脏保养。杰森是忠诚的朋友和书籍爱好者。他们感觉很幸运能有这次重要经历。搬箱子的男子气概！*
