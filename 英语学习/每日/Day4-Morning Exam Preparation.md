# Day 4 · CET-4 · 清晨备考

## Today's Scenario
> *Li Ming wakes up **early** on exam day. He looks at the **earth** outside his window, checks the clock — it's **eight** o'clock. He hurts his **elbow** while rushing, and his **elder** brother encourages him. The class **elects** a monitor, but the exam hall feels **empty**. His biggest **enemy** is his own anxiety. He must **enjoy** the learning process. When he **enters** the classroom, every **entry** of knowledge matters. **Equal** opportunities await. He **equips** himself with confidence. He **erects** his posture. Every small **error** teaches a lesson. The **essay** question is tough. Every **event** in life prepares him. He gives his **exact** best and **exerts** all his effort.*  
> *李明在考试日早早起床。他看着窗外的土地，看钟——八点了。匆忙中撞到肘部，哥哥鼓励他。班级选举班长，考场却显得空荡荡的。他最大的敌人是自己的焦虑。他必须享受学习的过程。走进教室，每一条知识条目都很重要。平等的机会在等待。他用自信装备自己，挺直腰杆。每个小错误都是教训。作文题很难。生活中的每个事件都在为他做准备。他全力以赴。*
