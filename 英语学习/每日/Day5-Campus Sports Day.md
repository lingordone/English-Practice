## 1. exile

<font color=purple>┃</font> 流放，放逐 n./v.

/ˈɛksaɪl/
![[audio/exile.mp3]]

### Example Sentences
1. *"The team captain felt like an exile after being removed from the competition."* — 队长被取消比赛资格后，感觉自己像被流放了一样。
   ![[audio/exile_ex1.mp3]]
2. *"He was exiled from the sports team for breaking the rules."* — 他因违反规定被逐出运动队。
   ![[audio/exile_ex2.mp3]]

### Dialogue
**A:** Why isn't Li Wei playing today?  
**B:** He broke the rules, so he's been exiled from the team.

### Grammar & Usage Note
- **exile sb. from**: 把某人从…流放/逐出。
  - √ *"He was exiled from the team for cheating."* — 正确用法。
  - × *"He exiled the team."* — 错误，exile的对象是人不是团队。

### Quiz
- **Fill in the blank**: "After the fight, he was ___ from the school."
  - Answer: **exiled**

## Scenario Dialogue

> **A:** Hey, are you ready for the sports day?  
> **B:** Almost! I've been doing extra training every morning.  
> **A:** I have faith in our team. We fight together!  
> **B:** Exactly. It's not just about winning — the team spirit exists in every player.  
> **A:** Remember the fable the coach told us? Slow and steady wins the race.  
> **B:** Haha, no fairy tales now — this is real competition! One fatal fault could cost us everything.  
> **A:** Don't worry. Everyone is doing their best. Look at the field — it's packed!  
> **B:** I can hear the crowd from behind the fence. The fever is real!  
> **A:** I feel a bit faint from all the excitement. Can you fetch me some water?  
> **B:** Sure. And after the games, there's a feast in the cafeteria!  
> **A:** Great! We need to eat lots of fibre to recover. Last year we finished fifth — this year let's aim for first!  
> **B:** Fifty teams are competing. The odds are tough, but nothing is impossible.  
> **A:** If anyone gets injured, they might be exiled from the team. So be careful!  
> **B:** I will. And I hope the judges favor us this time.  
> **A:** That fancy trophy is waiting for us. Let's go!  
> **B:** Let's fight till the end!
