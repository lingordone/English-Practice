## Scenario Dialogue

> **A:** Hey, you look exhausted! What happened?  
> **B:** Ugh, it's been a rough morning. First, I had to **cross** the main road during rush hour — there was such a **dense** **crowd** of people everywhere!  
> **A:** I know what you mean. The morning traffic is a real **curse**. My bus was **delayed** by almost twenty minutes.  
> **B:** At least you took the bus. I **cycle** to school, but the road **curves** so much near the campus that I almost fell off!  
> **A:** That sounds dangerous. Did you **crush** your phone or something?
