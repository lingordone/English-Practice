## 1. close

<font color=purple>┃</font> 关，闭；结束 动词

/kloʊz/
![[audio/close.mp3]]

### Example Sentences

**B:** Yes, I closed it. But we should also close the curtains.

## 8. could

<font color=purple>┃</font> (can的过去式)能，可以 情态动词

/kʊd/
![[audio/could.mp3]]

### Example Sentences
1. *"When I was young, I could run very fast."* — 我年轻时能跑得非常快。
   ![[audio/could_ex1.mp3]]
2. *"Could you please help me carry this box?"* — 你能帮我搬这个箱子吗？
   ![[audio/could_ex2.mp3]]

### Dialogue
**A:** Could you tell me where the library is?  
**B:** Sure, it's just around the corner.

### Grammar & Usage Note
- **could 表示委婉请求**: 用 could 比用 can 更礼貌。
  - √ *"Could you pass me the salt, please?"* — 请把盐递给我好吗？（礼貌）
  - √ *"Can you pass me the salt?"* — 能把盐递给我吗？（直接）
  - × *"Could I goes to the restroom?"* — could 后动词用原形，不能说 "goes"。

### Quiz
- **Fill in the blank**: "___ you please open the window? It's too hot."
  - Answer: **Could**

## Scenario Dialogue

> **A:** Hey, did you close the library door last night?  
> **B:** Yes, I did. But I noticed a piece of cloth on the ground near the old building.  
> **A:** Strange. A dark cloud was hanging over the campus too. Could it be related to the mystery?  
> **B:** Our basketball coach mentioned he saw someone creeping around there after practice.  
> **A:** That sounds crazy! Let's count the clues. We have cloth, a cloudy night, and a suspicious person.  
> **B:** And there's a crack in the wall of the old classroom. I saw workers with a crane nearby.
