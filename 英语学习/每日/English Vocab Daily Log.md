  - × *"I have a bag red."* — 形容词要放在名词前面。

## 4. white

### Pronunciation
/waɪt/
![[Colors Around Us/white.mp3]]

### Meaning
白色 (adj/n)

### Synonyms
- ivory — 象牙白
- cream — 奶油色

### Antonyms
- black — 黑色
- dark — 深色

### Collocations
- white snow — 白雪
- white shirt — 白衬衫

### Example Sentences
1. *"Snow is white."* — 雪是白色的。
   ![[Colors Around Us/white_ex1.mp3]]
2. *"The cat is white."* — 那只猫是白色的。
   ![[Colors Around Us/white_ex2.mp3]]

### Dialogue

**B:** Snow is white.  
**A:** Is the cat white too?  
**B:** Yes, the cat is white and very cute.

### Grammar & Usage Note
- **is / are**: 单数主语用 is，复数主语用 are。
  - √ *"Snow is white."* — 雪是白色的。（snow 不可数，用 is）
  - √ *"Clouds are white."* — 云是白色的。（clouds 复数，用 are）

### Quiz
- **Fill in the blank**: "Snow is ___ ."
  - Answer: **white**

## 5. black

### Pronunciation
/blæk/
![[Colors Around Us/black.mp3]]

### Meaning
黑色 (adj/n)

### Synonyms
- dark — 深色，黑暗
- jet-black — 乌黑的

### Antonyms
- white — 白色
- light — 浅色

### Collocations
- black car — 黑色的车
- black shoes — 黑鞋子

### Example Sentences
1. *"The car is black."* — 这辆车是黑色的。
   ![[Colors Around Us/black_ex1.mp3]]
2. *"I have black shoes."* — 我有黑色的鞋子。
   ![[Colors Around Us/black_ex2.mp3]]

### Dialogue
**A:** What color is your car?  
**B:** My car is black.  
**A:** Black is a cool color.  
**B:** Yes, I like black because it looks nice.

### Grammar & Usage Note
- **black vs. dark**: Black 是一种颜色（黑色），dark 是描述亮度（暗的）。
  - √ *"He wears a black jacket."* — 他穿一件黑色夹克。
  - √ *"The room is dark."* — 房间很暗。（不是颜色，是亮度）

### Quiz
- **Fill in the blank**: "I have ___ shoes."
  - Answer: **black**

### Shadowing Sentence
> *"The car is black."*  
> ![[Colors Around Us/black_shadow.mp3]]
