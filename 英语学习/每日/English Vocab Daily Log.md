## 1. hello

### Pronunciation
/həˈloʊ/
![[Meeting Someone New/hello.mp3]]

### Meaning
你好 (interjection)

### Synonyms
- hi
- hey

### Antonyms
- goodbye
- bye

### Collocations
- say hello — 打招呼
- hello, how are you — 你好，你好吗

### Example Sentences
1. *"Hello! How are you?"* — 你好！你好吗？
   ![[Meeting Someone New/hello_ex1.mp3]]
2. *"I say hello to my friend."* — 我跟朋友打招呼。
   ![[Meeting Someone New/hello_ex2.mp3]]

### Dialogue
**A:** Hello! My name is Tom.  
**B:** Hello, Tom! I am Lucy.  
**A:** Nice to meet you, Lucy.  
**B:** Nice to meet you too!

### Grammar & Usage Note
- **hello vs. hi**: hello 更正式/礼貌，hi 更随意。初次见面用 hello 比较合适。
  - √ *"Hello! My name is Sarah."* — 你好！我叫 Sarah。（礼貌，适合第一次见面）
  - × *"Hey! Gimme your name."* — 嘿！告诉我你名字。（太随意，不礼貌）

### Quiz
- **Fill in the blank**: "___! How are you today?"
  - Answer: **Hello**

## 2. name

### Pronunciation
/neɪm/
![[Meeting Someone New/name.mp3]]

### Meaning
名字 (noun)

### Synonyms
- first name
- nickname

### Antonyms
- (none)

### Collocations
- my name is — 我的名字是
- what is your name — 你叫什么名字

### Example Sentences
1. *"My name is Li Ming."* — 我的名字是李明。
   ![[Meeting Someone New/name_ex1.mp3]]
2. *"What is your name?"* — 你叫什么名字？
   ![[Meeting Someone New/name_ex2.mp3]]

### Dialogue
**A:** What is your name?  
**B:** My name is Sarah.  
**A:** That is a nice name!  
**B:** Thank you!

### Grammar & Usage Note
- **name + is**: 名字前面用 is（My name is..., His name is..., Her name is...）。名字是单数，所以用 is，不用 are。
  - √ *"My name is Jack."* — 我叫 Jack。
  - × *"My name are Jack."* — 错误（name 是单数，用 is 不是 are）

### Quiz
- **Fill in the blank**: "My ___ is Anna."
  - Answer: **name**

### Shadowing Sentence
> *"My name is Jack. What is your name?"*  
> ![[Meeting Someone New/name_shadow.mp3]]

## 3. nice

### Pronunciation
/naɪs/
![[Meeting Someone New/nice.mp3]]

### Meaning
令人愉快的，好的 (adjective)

### Synonyms
- good
- kind

### Antonyms
- bad
- mean

### Collocations
- nice to meet you — 很高兴认识你
- a nice day — 美好的一天

### Example Sentences
1. *"It is a nice day!"* — 今天天气真好！
   ![[Meeting Someone New/nice_ex1.mp3]]
2. *"You are very nice."* — 你非常友善。
   ![[Meeting Someone New/nice_ex2.mp3]]

### Dialogue
**A:** You are a nice friend.  
**B:** Thank you! You are nice too.  
**A:** Let's play together!  
**B:** Yes! That is nice.

### Grammar & Usage Note
- **nice + noun / be + nice**: nice 是形容词，放在名词前面（a nice day），或者放在 be 动词后面（you are nice）。
  - √ *"It is a nice day."* — 今天是美好的一天。
  - × *"It is a day nice."* — 错误（形容词放在名词前面）

### Quiz
- **Fill in the blank**: "It is a ___ day!"
  - Answer: **nice**

### Shadowing Sentence
> *"Nice to meet you! You are very nice."*  
> ![[Meeting Someone New/nice_shadow.mp3]]

## 4. meet

### Pronunciation
/miːt/
![[Meeting Someone New/meet.mp3]]

### Meaning
遇见，认识 (verb)

### Synonyms
- see
- know (someone)

### Antonyms
- leave
- miss

### Collocations
- nice to meet you — 很高兴认识你
- meet a friend — 见朋友

### Example Sentences
1. *"I meet my friend at school."* — 我在学校见我的朋友。
   ![[Meeting Someone New/meet_ex1.mp3]]
2. *"Nice to meet you!"* — 很高兴认识你！
   ![[Meeting Someone New/meet_ex2.mp3]]

### Dialogue
**A:** Do you meet your friend today?  
**B:** Yes, I meet her at the park.  
**A:** Is she nice?  
**B:** Yes, she is very nice!

### Grammar & Usage Note
- **meet + someone**: meet 后面直接加人（meet my friend, meet you）。不需要介词。
  - √ *"I meet my friend at school."* — 我在学校见我朋友。
  - × *"I meet with my friend at school."* — 对 beginners 来说，先用简单的 "meet"，不加 "with"。

### Quiz
- **Fill in the blank**: "Nice to ___ you!"
  - Answer: **meet**

### Shadowing Sentence
> *"I meet my friend at the park. She is nice."*

## 5. friend

### Pronunciation
/frend/
![[Meeting Someone New/friend.mp3]]

### Meaning
朋友 (noun)

### Synonyms
- buddy
- pal

### Antonyms
- enemy
- stranger

### Collocations
- my friend — 我的朋友
- make a friend — 交朋友

### Example Sentences
1. *"She is my good friend."* — 她是我的好朋友。
   ![[Meeting Someone New/friend_ex1.mp3]]
2. *"I make a new friend today."* — 我今天交了一个新朋友。
   ![[Meeting Someone New/friend_ex2.mp3]]

### Dialogue
**A:** Is she your friend?  
**B:** Yes, she is my best friend.  
**A:** How do you know her?  
**B:** We meet at school. She is very nice!

### Grammar & Usage Note
- **friend + is/are**: 一个朋友用 is（My friend is nice.），两个以上用 are（My friends are nice.）。
  - √ *"She is my friend."* — 她是我的朋友。
  - × *"She are my friend."* — 错误（she 用 is，不用 are）

### Quiz
- **Fill in the blank**: "She is my good ___."
  - Answer: **friend**

### Shadowing Sentence
> *"My friend is nice. I like my friend."*  
> ![[Meeting Someone New/friend_shadow.mp3]]
