## 1. rice

### Pronunciation
/raɪs/
![[At Breakfast/rice.mp3]]

### Meaning
米饭 (noun)

### Synonyms
- grain — 谷物
- food — 食物

### Antonyms
- meat — 肉

### Collocations (常用搭配)
- a bowl of rice — 一碗米饭
- eat rice — 吃米饭

### Example Sentences
1. *"I eat rice for lunch."* — 我午饭吃米饭。
   ![[At Breakfast/rice_ex1.mp3]]
2. *"Rice is white and soft."* — 米饭是白色的，很软。
   ![[At Breakfast/rice_ex2.mp3]]

### Dialogue
**A:** Do you eat rice every day?  
**B:** Yes, I eat rice for lunch and dinner.  
**A:** I like rice too. It is very tasty!  
**B:** Yes, rice is my favorite food.

### Grammar & Usage Note
- **rice is uncountable**: 在英语中，rice 是不可数名词。不能说 "a rice" 或 "rices"。
  - √ *"I eat rice every day."* — 我每天吃米饭。
  - × *"I eat a rice every day."* — 错误

### Quiz
- **Fill in the blank**: "I eat ___ for lunch."
  - Answer: **rice**

### Shadowing Sentence
> *"I eat rice for lunch. Rice is white and soft."*

## 4. egg

### Pronunciation
/ɛɡ/
![[At Breakfast/egg.mp3]]

### Meaning
鸡蛋 (noun)

### Synonyms
- food — 食物
- breakfast — 早餐

### Antonyms
- (none)

### Collocations (常用搭配)
- an egg — 一个鸡蛋
- eat an egg — 吃鸡蛋

### Example Sentences
1. *"I eat an egg for breakfast."* — 我早餐吃一个鸡蛋。
   ![[At Breakfast/egg_ex1.mp3]]
2. *"The egg is round and yellow."* — 鸡蛋是圆圆的，黄黄的。
   ![[At Breakfast/egg_ex2.mp3]]

### Dialogue
**A:** How do you like your egg?  
**B:** I like my egg fried.  
**A:** I like boiled eggs.  
**B:** Both are good!

### Grammar & Usage Note
- **egg is countable**: egg 是可数名词！可以说 "an egg"、"two eggs"。
  - √ *"I eat an egg for breakfast."* — 我早餐吃一个鸡蛋。
  - √ *"I eat two eggs every day."* — 我每天吃两个鸡蛋。
  - × *"I eat egg for breakfast."* (应该说 an egg 或 eggs)

### Quiz
- **Fill in the blank**: "I eat ___ egg for breakfast."
  - Answer: **an**

### Shadowing Sentence
