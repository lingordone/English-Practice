# Day 7 · CET-4 · 校园早晨

## Today's Scenario
> A university student goes through a typical morning — waking up, heading to the cafeteria, walking to class, and chatting with friends on campus. The morning starts with a sharp frost, passes by the library's glass front, and ends with a funny joke in class.  
> 一个大学生经历了一个典型的早晨 — 起床、去食堂、走向教室、和朋友们在校园里聊天。早晨从霜冻开始，经过图书馆的玻璃大门，以课堂上的一句玩笑结束。

## 1. found

<font color=purple>┃</font> 创办，建立；找到（find的过去式） v.

/faʊnd/
![[audio/found.mp3]]

### Example Sentences
1. *"Our university was **found**ed in 1950 and has a long history."* — 我们大学创办于1950年，历史悠久。
   ![[audio/found_ex1.mp3]]
2. *"She decided to **found** a student club for English learners."* — 她决定为英语学习者创办一个学生社团。
   ![[audio/found_ex2.mp3]]

### Dialogue
**A:** Do you know when our school was **found**ed?  
**B:** I think it was **found**ed in the 1980s. Let me check online.

### Grammar & Usage Note
- **found** (v.) + 机构/组织：表示"创办"。注意和 **find** (找到) 过去式 **found** 拼写相同，但含义不同。
  - √ *"They **found**ed a new research center."* — 正确，创办。
  - × *"They **found** a new research center last year."* — 歧义（"found"可以是find的过去式，也可能是"创办"原形）。根据上下文判断。

### Quiz
- **Fill in the blank**: "The school was ______ in 2001 by a group of teachers."
  - Answer: **founded**
