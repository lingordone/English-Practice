### 🗣️ Dialogue
- **Sophia:** My suitcase is too heavy. Is yours **light** enough to help carry some of my stuff?
- **Jason:** Yeah, mine's pretty **light**. Let me take a few of your books.

### 📖 Grammar & Usage Note
- **light (adj)**: 轻的；明亮的；浅色的（比较级 lighter, 最高级 lightest）
  - √ *"Feathers are very **light** and float in the air."* — 羽毛很轻，能在空中漂浮。
  - × *"This box is **light** enough for one person to carry."* — 这个箱子足够轻，一个人就能搬动。

### ❓ Quiz
- **Fill in the blank**: "Feathers are very ___ ."
  - Answer: **light**

## 🎭 Scenario Dialogue
- **Sophia:** Hey Jason, could you help me with this apartment **lease**? I want to make sure everything is **legal**.
- **Jason:** Of course! But first, let me **knock** on the neighbor's door to borrow a **knife** for opening these boxes.
- **Sophia:** Good thinking. Then we need to **label** every box so we know what's inside each **layer**.
- **Jason:** At **least** let me **kneel** down and check under the bed for anything we missed.
- **Sophia:** Don't make me **laugh**! You'll find nothing but dust under there. But check the **level** of the floor, it might not be **large** enough.
- **Jason:** I need to **learn** how to use this **laser** printer we borrowed. Also, is there any **juice** or **lemon** water in the fridge?
- **Sophia:** Yes, I bought some apple **juice** yesterday. I'll see you **later** — I need to **leave** for class in 5 minutes.
- **Jason:** No problem. This **light** backpack is easy to carry. And this **lever** handle on the cabinet is stuck again!
- **Sophia:** Don't **judge** the dorm too harshly — it's old but it's home.
- **Jason:** You're right. Let's get packing!
