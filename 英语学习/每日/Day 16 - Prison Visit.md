## 1. prisoner

### Pronunciation
/ˈprɪzənər/
![[Prison Visit/prisoner.mp3]]

### Meaning
囚犯，犯人 (noun)

### Synonyms
- inmate — 囚犯（同义，更正式）
- convict — 已定罪的人

### Antonyms
- guard — 警卫
- visitor — 访客

### Collocations
- visit a prisoner — 探视囚犯
- the prisoner is… — 囚犯是……

### Example Sentences
1. *"The prisoner is waiting in the visiting room."* — 囚犯正在探视室等待。
   ![[Prison Visit/prisoner_ex1.mp3]]
2. *"She visits the prisoner every Sunday."* — 她每个星期天都来探望这名囚犯。
   ![[Prison Visit/prisoner_ex2.mp3]]

### Dialogue
**Guard:** Are you here to visit a prisoner?  
**Visitor:** Yes, I am here to see my friend.  
**Guard:** Please sign the form first.  
**Visitor:** OK. Is the prisoner ready?  
**Guard:** Yes, he is waiting in the visiting room.

### Grammar & Usage Note
- **prisoner + is/are**: 一个囚犯用 is（The prisoner is waiting.），多个用 are（The prisoners are in their cells.）。
  - √ *"The prisoner is in the visiting room."* — 囚犯在探视室。
  - × *"The prisoner are in the visiting room."* — 错误（prisoner 是单数，用 is）

### Quiz
- **Fill in the blank**: "The ___ is waiting in the visiting room."
  - Answer: **prisoner**

### Shadowing Sentence
> *"The prisoner is waiting in the visiting room."*

## 2. proper

### Pronunciation
/ˈprɑːpər/
![[Prison Visit/proper.mp3]]

### Meaning
适当的，正确的 (adjective)

### Synonyms
- correct — 正确的
- right — 对的

### Antonyms
- improper — 不适当的
- wrong — 错误的

### Collocations
- proper identification — 有效证件
- proper procedure — 正确程序

### Example Sentences
1. *"You need proper identification to enter."* — 进入需要出示有效证件。
   ![[Prison Visit/proper_ex1.mp3]]
2. *"The guard follows proper procedure."* — 警卫遵循正确的程序。
   ![[Prison Visit/proper_ex2.mp3]]

### Dialogue
**Guard:** Do you have proper identification?  
**Visitor:** Yes, here is my ID card.  
**Guard:** Thank you. This is the proper procedure.  
**Visitor:** I understand. I want to do it the right way.

### Grammar & Usage Note
- **proper + noun**: proper 是形容词，放在名词前面修饰名词。
  - √ *"Please show your proper ID."* — 请出示你的有效证件。
  - × *"Please show your ID proper."* — 错误（形容词应放在名词前面）

### Quiz
- **Fill in the blank**: "You need ___ identification to enter."
  - Answer: **proper**

### Shadowing Sentence
> *"Please show your proper identification at the front desk."*  
> ![[Prison Visit/proper_shadow.mp3]]

## 3. rubber

### Pronunciation
/ˈrʌbər/
![[Prison Visit/rubber.mp3]]

### Meaning
橡胶，橡皮 (noun)

### Synonyms
- eraser — 橡皮擦
- elastic — 弹性材料

### Antonyms
- (none direct) — 无直接反义词

### Collocations
- rubber stamp — 橡皮图章
- rubber gloves — 橡胶手套

### Example Sentences
1. *"The guard stamps the form with a rubber stamp."* — 警卫用橡皮图章在表格上盖章。
   ![[Prison Visit/rubber_ex1.mp3]]
2. *"He wears rubber gloves for the search."* — 他戴着橡胶手套进行搜查。
   ![[Prison Visit/rubber_ex2.mp3]]

### Dialogue
**Guard:** I need to stamp this form.  
**Visitor:** OK. What is that?  
**Guard:** It is a rubber stamp. It marks the time of your visit.  
**Visitor:** I see. Thank you.

### Grammar & Usage Note
- **rubber + noun**: rubber 可以作名词（This is rubber.），也可以作形容词放在名词前面（rubber stamp, rubber gloves）。
  - √ *"The guard uses a rubber stamp."* — 警卫使用橡皮图章。
  - √ *"The gloves are made of rubber."* — 这手套是橡胶做的。

### Quiz
- **Fill in the blank**: "The guard uses a ___ stamp."
  - Answer: **rubber**

### Shadowing Sentence
> *"The guard uses a rubber stamp on the paper."*  
> ![[Prison Visit/rubber_shadow.mp3]]

## Scenario Dialogue
> **Guard:** Hello, welcome to the prison. Are you here to visit a prisoner?  
> **Visitor:** Yes, I am here to see my brother.  
> **Guard:** Do you have proper identification?  
> **Visitor:** Yes, here is my ID card.  
> **Guard:** Please sign the visitor log.  
> **Visitor:** OK. Is there anything else?  
> **Guard:** I will stamp this form with a rubber stamp. Then you can go in.  
> **Visitor:** Thank you. How long can I stay?  
> **Guard:** 30 minutes. Please follow the proper procedure.  
> **Visitor:** I will. Thank you for your help.
